Target audience

Description:

Most of the audience is made up of adults, but youth might also check the website. Individuals who access the site are interested in learning more about the weather for that day and for the next days. They are also interested in community news and issues and are looking for a local trusthworthy newspaper that can help them be up to date with their communitie's issued.

Personas

1. Matt Jones -- Matt is in his 30s. His manages a construction team and spends most time outside. For him it is important to know the weather in other to best plan the schedule for the week and how to best protect his subordinates. He checks the website daily for updates on the weather and also to find our news about the community.

2. Marry Smith -- Marry is in her 60s. She is a widow and grandma. She spends her days with her grandchildren or gardening, but is also interested in the community. She checks the website for the weather and is also interested in the website's news. She enjoys finding out about the community and being involved in it.

Scenarious

  • How is the weather going to be this week?
  • Are we going to have fireworks for the 4th of July?
  • When is the opening of the new store in town?
  • Which are the new city regulations on house construction?
  • Are there any yellow weather codes for next week?
