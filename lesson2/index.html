Site purpose:

This website is meant for day to day information about the weather and other news from the community. It helps people be informed before they leave their house to know how to dress according to the weather and be informed about issues and news concerning their community or other communities. They can acces the website using their desktop computers, tablets or mobile phones. Thus, the purpose of the website is to inform community members on weather and other news about the community.
