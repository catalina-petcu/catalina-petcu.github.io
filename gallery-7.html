Working from home has increased in recent months

According to the last date published by the town hall on Wednesday, November 17, 2021, the number of people working from home has increased compared to the previous quarter of 2020. Though it was expected that the vaccine would reduce the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic on the work environment, there are no sign that work would go back to normal.

The majority of business that can funtion as employees work from home has decide to keep this practice as it has helped them save money they would have payed on rent, utilities, parking, etc. Most of employees have gotten used to this new way of working and have accepted happily this change as a long term practice.
